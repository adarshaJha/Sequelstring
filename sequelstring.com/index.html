What is Blockchain?

The Blockchain is an incorruptible digital ledger of economic transactions that can be programmed to record not just financial transactions but virtually everything of value.

How can Blockchain be useful?

Information held on a Blockchain exists as a shared and reconciled database. The Blockchain database isn’t stored in any single location, meaning the records it keeps are public and verifiable. There is no centralized version of this information exists throughout the world making it difficult for a hacker to corrupt.

How can I setup a private Blockchain?

We2Blocks comes with a very versatile and easy to configure product called We2Chain. You can setup your own private Blockchain and provide rules and regulations to govern the Blockchain. Contact us to know more about We2Chain.

Where to get started?

If you are not able to find the Blockchain application or solution for your need, no need to worry. We also offer indepth individual consulting for more specific and complex Blockchain issues. Our vast experience allows us to come up with best-practice solutions and innovative implementations.

What is an ICO?

Initial Coin Offering allows investors to support new initiatives using cryptocurrency and principles of crowdfunding. During an ICO’s crowdsale, participants purchase project-specific cryptocurrency tokens as a form of investment in the company. Just like an IPO, an ICO represents the first stage of a project going public.

What is a Pre-ICO?

Pre-ICOs for a firm is as a way to accrue funds to cater for the expenses incurred on the way to launching the main ICO. The fund-raising targets for Pre-ICOs are often lower as compared to that of the main ICO and tokens are usually sold cheaper. It usually caters for expenses like paid promo ads, strategic recruitment and meet-up costs to help whip up investor interests.

What is an ICO token?

ICO tokens are the cryptocurrency or crypto-token which are exchanged against cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin or Ethereum while raising funds or capital for the firm. There are 3 types of ICO tokens: Equity, Securities and Utility. For more information, do contact us.

How much time does it take to run an ICO?

It’s a 3 week of procedure to launch an ICO, including the token development. However, the time differs based on the customization pre-sale and sale smart contracts. This might be extended as per goals of the firm.

What are Smart Contracts?

Smart contracts help you exchange money, property, shares, or anything of value in a transparent, conflict-free way while avoiding the services of a middleman.

How can I use Smart Contracts?

Smart Contracts can be used all across the chain from financial services to healthcare to insurance. For example in voting systems Smart Contracts can be used to provide an infinitely more secure system with ledger protected votes. For more information on Smart Contracts, do contact us.

How to create Smart Contracts?

If you are struggling with Smart Contracts, need not to worry. We2Blocks provide indepth consultancy and development of Smart Contracts on major Blockchain networks. Our team of experts with guide you to a near perfect implementation of Smart Contracts.

What Smart Contracts Provide me?

Smart Contracts provides you with Autonomy, Trust, Backup, Safety, Speed, Savings and Accuracy. In whole Smart Contracts guarantee a very specific set of outcomes. There’s never any confusion and there’s never any need for litigation.

What is a Cryptocurrency exchange?

Cryptocurrency exchanges are online platforms where you can exchange one cryptocurrency for another cryptocurrency (or for fiat currency). They can be market makers that typically take the bid/ask spreads as transaction commissions for their services or simply charge fees as a matching platform.

Why to use Cryptocurrency exchange?

Cryptocurrency exchange provides you with a trade engine where you can buy and sell owned Cryptocurrencies. It helps you in liquidity, funds management and order matching to get you the best price avaialble.

Are Cryptocurrency exchange safe?

The most convenient thing to do is to leave your coins on the exchanges. If you are an active trader, keeping your coins on the exchanges allows you to transact and react to market conditions quite quickly. Keep your private key safe and you would be well protected.

What are some of the famous Cryptocurrency exchange?

Some of the famous exchanges are Binance, Bitmex, Gate.io, YoBit, Bibox, Changelly. You can securely use them to buy and sell Cryptocurrency.
